After sessions finish, Convo analyzes the transcripts and recordings to produce your results.

## Triggering analysis

Hit **Run Analysis** from your study dashboard once sessions are done.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The study dashboard showing the Run Analysis button and session completion status]
</Frame>

<Info>
  You can run analysis after each session or wait until all sessions are done. Analyzing everything together gives you better cross-session insights.
</Info>

---

## Pipeline stages

Analysis goes through five stages:

| Stage | What's happening |
|-------|-------------|
| **Pending** | Queued, waiting to start |
| **Preparing** | Processing transcripts and recordings |
| **Analyzing** | Finding themes, pulling quotes, generating insights |
| **Generating clips** | Creating video clips from recordings |
| **Complete** | Everything's ready |

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The analysis progress indicator showing the pipeline stages]
</Frame>

Usually takes a few minutes, depending on how many sessions you have. You'll get notified when it's done.

---

## What you get

Convo produces five types of results:

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Summary" icon="file-lines" href="/results/summary">
    Findings, decisions, agreements, and debates. Includes metrics like consensus rate and participant count.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deep Dive & Responses" icon="list-check" href="/results/deep-dive-and-responses">
    Moment-by-moment analysis and per-participant views.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Themes" icon="layer-group" href="/results/themes">
    Patterns across all sessions with supporting quotes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Video Clips" icon="film" href="/results/video-clips">
    Clips of key moments from your sessions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Explore" icon="comments" href="/results/explore">
    Chat interface to ask questions about your data and generate charts.
  </Card>
</Columns>

**Summary** is the big picture. **Deep Dive** and **Responses** go moment-by-moment. **Themes** show patterns across participants. **Clips** give you shareable video. **Explore** lets you ask follow-up questions.
