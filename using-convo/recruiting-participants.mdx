You have two paths to get participants into your study: use our built-in recruitment panels or share a direct link with your own participants.

## Use our panels

Convo offers integrated recruitment panels so you can find participants without leaving the platform.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The panel recruitment interface showing audience targeting options and participant count settings]
</Frame>

### General population

General population panels are available directly in Convo. Set your target audience criteria, specify how many participants you need, and launch recruitment. Participants are sourced, screened, and scheduled automatically.

### Professional and B2B recruitment

Need to reach specific professional audiences -- decision-makers, developers, healthcare workers, or other specialized groups? [Contact our team](mailto:support@useconvo.ai) to discuss B2B recruitment options.

<Info>
  When using our panels, participants still go through your screener. The panel provides the initial pool; your screener narrows it to qualified participants.
</Info>

## Bring your own participants

Already have a participant list? Share a direct link and let them self-enroll.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The direct link sharing interface showing the unique study URL and copy button]
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Copy your study link">
    Go to the Recruitment tab in your study and copy the unique participant link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the link">
    Send the link to your participants via email, Slack, social media, or any other channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Participants sign up">
    Participants click the link, complete your screener, pass a video and audio check, and are scheduled into a session.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Participant funnel

Every participant goes through the same funnel, regardless of how they were recruited:

| Stage | What happens |
|-------|-------------|
| **New** | Participant has accessed the study link |
| **Screener** | Participant is completing the screener questions |
| **Video check** | Participant is verifying their camera and microphone work |
| **Qualified** | Participant passed the screener and video check |
| **Signed up** | Participant is confirmed and scheduled for a session |
| **Joined** | Participant has entered and completed a session |

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The recruitment dashboard showing participant counts at each funnel stage]
</Frame>

You can monitor the funnel in real time from the Recruitment tab of your study.

## Scheduling recruitment rounds

You can run recruitment in rounds. Each round lets you specify how many additional participants you need. This is useful when:

- You want to start with a small pilot before scaling up
- Initial sessions reveal you need more diversity in your sample
- You are running a longitudinal study across multiple weeks

<Tip>
  Start with a small round (3-5 participants) to validate your screener and discussion guide before recruiting the full sample.
</Tip>
