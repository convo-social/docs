Once participants are recruited and scheduled, Convo takes over. The AI moderator runs each session following your discussion guide while the Director Agent monitors things behind the scenes.

## How AI moderation works

The AI moderator speaks to participants in natural, conversational language. During a session, it:

- Welcomes everyone and explains how the session works
- Works through your discussion guide moment by moment
- Asks follow-up questions based on what participants actually say
- Handles turn-taking so everyone gets heard
- Shows stimulus materials when a moment calls for them
- Wraps up and thanks participants

The AI moderator adapts on the fly. If someone raises something relevant to a later topic, it can rearrange the order. If a high-priority moment needs more time, it'll spend less on the lower-priority ones.

## Session stages

Every session goes through five stages:

| Stage | What happens |
|-------|-------------|
| **Waiting room** | Participants join and wait. The AI moderator starts once enough people are in. |
| **Intro** | The AI moderator introduces itself, explains the format, sets expectations. |
| **Discussion** | The main part. The AI moderator works through your guide, asks questions, facilitates conversation. |
| **Conclusion** | The AI moderator summarizes, asks for final thoughts, thanks everyone. |
| **Ended** | Session's over. Recordings and transcripts are saved. |

---

## The Director Agent

Behind the scenes, a secondary AI called the Director Agent monitors each session and makes real-time calls:

- Tracks remaining time and tells the AI moderator when to move on or go deeper
- Makes sure all high-priority moments get covered
- Flags audio or video quality drops
- Can reorder moments based on how the conversation is going

You don't interact with the Director Agent. It runs automatically.

---

## Multi-participant handling

Convo is built for group sessions. The AI moderator uses speaker identification to know who's talking. It can:

- Direct questions to specific people
- Invite quieter participants to share
- Handle overlapping speech
- Reference what someone said earlier in the conversation

<Info>
  Each participant's contributions are individually attributed in transcripts and analysis.
</Info>

## Stimulus presentation

If you attached media to any moments in your guide, the AI moderator shows them automatically when it gets to that moment. Participants see the stimulus on their screen while the discussion continues.

## Video and audio check

Before joining, every participant does an AI-powered video and audio check:

- Camera working, face visible
- Mic picking up clear audio
- Internet connection stable enough for video

If something fails, they're prompted to fix it before they can join.

## What participants see

From the participant side:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join page">
    They click the session link and see a branded join page with session details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Device settings">
    Select camera and mic, complete the video/audio check.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Waiting room">
    Wait for the session to start. They can see how many others have joined.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The session">
    the AI moderator runs the session. Participants see the AI moderator's avatar, other participants' video feeds, and any stimulus materials. They talk naturally, like a video call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Session ends">
    the AI moderator wraps up. Participants see a thank-you screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>
