title: What is Convo?
description: AI-moderated focus groups that run themselves. Qualitative insights in hours, not weeks.
---

Convo is an AI-moderated focus group platform. You design a discussion guide, recruit participants, and our AI moderator runs live video sessions with them. When sessions wrap up, Convo analyzes the transcripts and gives you summaries, themes, and video clips.

No scheduling. No moderator bias. No weeks of manual coding.

## Who it's for

- **UX researchers** who want qualitative depth without the timeline
- **Product managers** validating ideas with real users before building
- **Marketers** who need actual voice-of-customer, not survey data
- **Insights teams** running research programs that need to scale

## How it works

1. **Design your study** with the AI Study Builder or manual editor.
2. **Recruit participants** through our panels or share a direct link.
3. **Sessions run on their own.** The AI moderator runs live video sessions with your participants.
4. **Get results.** Summaries, themes, and video clips, ready to share.
