title: What is Convo?
description: AI-moderated focus groups that run themselves. Get rich qualitative insights in hours, not weeks.
---

Convo is an AI-moderated focus group platform. You design a discussion guide, recruit participants, and our AI moderator -- Peggy -- runs live video sessions with your participants. When the sessions finish, Convo automatically analyzes everything and gives you summaries, themes, video clips, and a shareable trailer.

No scheduling headaches. No moderator bias. No weeks of manual analysis.

## Who is Convo for?

- **UX researchers** who need qualitative depth at quantitative speed
- **Product managers** who want to validate ideas with real users before building
- **Marketers** looking for authentic voice-of-customer insights
- **Insights teams** running recurring research programs at scale

## How it works

1. **Design your study** -- Use the AI Study Builder or manual editor to create your discussion guide and screener.
2. **Recruit participants** -- Use our recruitment panels or bring your own participants via a direct link.
3. **Sessions run automatically** -- Peggy moderates live video sessions with your participants.
4. **Get results** -- Convo analyzes everything and delivers summaries, themes, clips, and a shareable trailer.
