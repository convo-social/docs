Convo runs focus groups for you. You write a discussion guide (or let our AI build one), recruit participants, and we handle the rest. Sessions run automatically with an AI moderator. Analysis is done when you wake up the next morning.

The whole process — from "I have a question" to "here's what customers think" — takes hours, not weeks.

## Who it's for

- **UX researchers** who want qualitative depth without the timeline
- **Product managers** validating ideas with real users before building
- **Founders** who need to hear from customers before making a big bet
- **Marketers** who want what customers actually say, not what they check on a survey
- **Insights teams** who need to run research repeatedly without scaling headcount

## How Convo works

The AI study builder creates a discussion guide from a description of what you want to learn. Takes a few minutes. You can also build one manually or upload an existing guide.

Once your guide is ready, recruit participants through our panels or share a link with your own. The AI moderator runs live multi-participant video sessions, asks follow-ups, manages turn-taking. You don't need to be there.

After sessions wrap, Convo analyzes the transcripts and gives you themes, quotes, agreements, debates, and video clips of the best moments. No manual coding. Share the clips with your team or drop the findings into a deck.
