Convo helps you deeply understand your customers without the usual friction. You go from a research question to real insights in a matter of hours.

The AI-assisted study builder helps you create a discussion guide in minutes. From there, Convo handles the manual work — recruiting, scheduling, and running focus groups — automatically. A few hours later, you have themes, quotes, video clips, and a full analysis ready to share.

## Who it's for

- **UX researchers** who want qualitative depth without the timeline
- **Product managers** validating ideas with real users before building
- **Founders** who need to hear directly from customers before making big calls
- **Marketers** who need actual voice-of-customer, not survey data
- **Insights teams** running research programs that need to scale

## What makes Convo different

- **Question to insights in hours.** Build a guide in minutes with the AI study builder, run sessions the same day, get analyzed results hours later.
- **Fully automated focus groups.** The AI moderator runs live multi-participant sessions, handles turn-taking, asks follow-ups, and keeps the conversation on track. You don't need to be there.
- **Automatic analysis.** Convo pulls themes, quotes, agreements, and debates from the transcripts. No manual coding.
- **Shareable video clips.** AI-generated clips of the best moments. Send them to stakeholders without touching a video editor.

## How it works

1. **Build your guide** with the AI study builder or manual editor.
2. **Recruit participants** through our panels or share a direct link.
3. **Sessions run automatically.** The AI moderator runs live video sessions with your participants.
4. **Get results.** Summaries, themes, and video clips, ready to share.
