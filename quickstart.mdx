This walks you through the full Convo workflow. By the end, you'll have a completed study with insights and video clips.

<Frame>
  [VIDEO: Full platform walkthrough. Record a Loom showing the complete flow from creating a study through to viewing results. Aim for 3-5 minutes.]
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your study">
    Log in to [Convo](https://convo.build) and click **New Study**. Two options:

    - **AI Study Builder** — describe your research goals and Convo generates a discussion guide, screener, and settings.
    - **Manual editor** — build everything yourself with the form-based editor.

    Either way, you end up with a discussion guide (topics Peggy will cover), a screener (to filter participants), and interview settings (duration, group size, etc.).

    <Info>
      The AI Study Builder is the fastest way to start. You can always edit the generated guide afterward.
    </Info>

    [More on setting up a study](/using-convo/setting-up-a-study)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Recruit participants">
    Two paths:

    - **Our panels** — general population participants are available directly in Convo. For professional or B2B recruitment, [contact our team](mailto:support@useconvo.ai).
    - **Bring your own** — share a direct link with your participant list. They'll go through the screener and video check on their own.

    Once participants qualify, they're scheduled into sessions automatically.

    [More on recruitment](/using-convo/recruiting-participants)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sessions run on their own">
    Peggy welcomes participants, walks them through your discussion topics, and wraps up. You don't need to be there.

    Sessions are recorded and transcribed in real time. The Director Agent monitors quality behind the scenes and adjusts the flow if needed.

    [More on live sessions](/using-convo/live-sessions)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Analyze results">
    After sessions finish, hit **Run Analysis** from your study dashboard. Convo processes the transcripts, finds patterns, and generates your results. Usually takes a few minutes.

    [More on analysis](/using-convo/analysis)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore your findings">
    Your results include:

    - **Summary** — findings, decisions, agreements, and debates across all sessions
    - **Deep Dive** — moment-by-moment breakdown of how each topic played out
    - **Themes** — patterns with supporting quotes
    - **Clips** — video highlights you can share with stakeholders
    - **Explore** — chat interface to ask questions about your data

    [Start with the Summary](/results/summary) or [jump into Explore](/results/explore)
  </Step>
</Steps>
