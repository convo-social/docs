This guide walks you through the full Convo workflow end-to-end. By the end, you will have a completed study with AI-generated insights, video clips, and a shareable trailer.

<Frame>
  [VIDEO: Full platform walkthrough -- record a Loom showing the complete flow from creating a study through to viewing results. Aim for 3-5 minutes.]
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your study">
    Log in to [Convo](https://app.useconvo.ai) and click **New Study**. You have two options:

    - **AI Study Builder** -- Describe your research goals in plain language and Convo generates a complete discussion guide, screener, and settings for you.
    - **Manual editor** -- Build everything yourself using the form-based editor.

    Either way, you will end up with a discussion guide (the topics Peggy will cover), a screener (to filter participants), and interview settings (duration, group size, etc.).

    <Info>
      The AI Study Builder is the fastest way to get started. You can always edit the generated guide manually afterward.
    </Info>

    [Learn more about setting up a study](/using-convo/setting-up-a-study)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Recruit participants">
    You have two paths to get participants into your study:

    - **Use our panels** -- Access general population participants directly within Convo. For professional or B2B recruitment, [contact our team](mailto:support@useconvo.ai).
    - **Bring your own** -- Share a direct link with your participant list. They will go through the screener and video check on their own.

    Once participants qualify, they are scheduled into sessions automatically.

    [Learn more about recruitment](/using-convo/recruiting-participants)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sessions run automatically">
    When a session starts, Peggy (the AI moderator) welcomes participants, guides them through your discussion topics, and wraps up the conversation. You do not need to be present.

    Sessions are recorded and transcribed in real time. The Director Agent works behind the scenes to monitor quality and adapt the session flow as needed.

    [Learn more about live sessions](/using-convo/live-sessions)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Analyze results">
    After sessions complete, trigger analysis from your study dashboard. Convo processes the transcripts, identifies patterns, and generates your results. This typically takes a few minutes depending on the number of sessions.

    [Learn more about analysis](/using-convo/analysis)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore your findings">
    Your results include:

    - **Summary** -- Key findings, decisions, agreements, and debates across all sessions
    - **Deep Dive** -- Moment-by-moment analysis of how each topic played out
    - **Themes** -- AI-identified patterns with supporting quotes
    - **Clips and Trailer** -- Auto-generated video highlights you can share with stakeholders
    - **Explore** -- An AI chat interface to ask questions about your study data

    [Start with the Summary](/results/summary) or [jump into Explore](/results/explore)
  </Step>
</Steps>
