Convo pulls the best moments from your session recordings and turns them into short video clips.

## How clips work

After analysis, you get a set of clips from across your sessions. Each one captures something worth sharing: a strong opinion, an emotional reaction, a debate between participants, a clear articulation of a finding.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The clips gallery showing a grid of auto-generated clips with thumbnails, participant names, and topic labels]
</Frame>

### Browsing

Clips are organized by moment. Browse all of them or filter by:

- Discussion topic
- Participant
- Theme

Each clip shows a thumbnail, who's speaking, and a short description.

### Previewing

Click any clip to play it inline. You'll see the video alongside the transcript for that segment.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The clip player showing a video clip with the transcript displayed alongside it]
</Frame>

### Processing stages

Clips go through a few stages:

- **Generating** — being created from the raw recording
- **Enhanced** — processed with better audio and visual quality
- **Ready** — available to view and share
