Convo pulls the best moments from your session recordings and turns them into short, shareable video clips.

## Why it matters

A 30-second clip of a customer explaining their frustration is worth more than a page of analysis. Clips let you share evidence with stakeholders who won't read a full report — drop them in a Slack thread, a deck, or a product brief.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The clips gallery showing clip thumbnails with speaker names, topic labels, and descriptions]
</Frame>

## What's in it

Convo automatically identifies moments worth clipping — strong opinions, emotional reactions, debates between participants, clear articulations of a finding — and generates short videos for each one.

Each clip includes:

- The video segment from the recording
- The transcript for that segment
- Who's speaking
- Which topic and theme it connects to

## Browsing and filtering

Clips are organized by moment. Browse all of them or filter by:

- **Discussion topic** — clips from a specific moment
- **Participant** — everything a specific person said
- **Theme** — clips that support a specific pattern

Click any clip to preview it inline with the transcript alongside.
