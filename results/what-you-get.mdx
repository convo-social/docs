After sessions finish, hit **Run Analysis** from your study dashboard. Convo processes everything — usually takes a few minutes. You'll get notified when it's ready.

<Frame>
  [VIDEO: Loom walkthrough of results — clicking Run Analysis, then a quick tour of each tab. ~3 minutes.]
</Frame>

<Info>
  You can run analysis after each session or wait until all sessions are done. Analyzing everything together gives you better cross-session insights.
</Info>

## Qualitative depth, quantitative data

Convo doesn't just give you quotes and themes. It generates quantitative data from your qualitative research — charts, metrics, consensus rates, sentiment breakdowns. You get the depth of a focus group with the data you'd expect from a survey.
