After sessions finish, hit **Run Analysis** from your study dashboard. Convo processes everything — usually takes a few minutes. You'll get notified when it's ready.

<Frame>
  [VIDEO: Loom walkthrough of results — clicking Run Analysis, then touring each tab. ~3 minutes.]
</Frame>

<Info>
  You can run analysis after each session or wait until all sessions are done. Analyzing everything together gives you better cross-session insights.
</Info>

---

## Summary

Where you go first. The big picture of what you learned.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The Summary view showing metrics, a decision card, and key findings]
</Frame>

- **Metrics** — participant count, session count, consensus rate, number of debates
- **Decision cards** — a clear verdict for each research objective with confidence level (High/Moderate/Low), evidence, and recommended next steps
- **Key findings** — headline insights with participant counts and quotes
- **Agreements** — topics where participants broadly aligned
- **Debates** — topics where participants genuinely disagreed, showing both sides with quotes
- **Emergent insights** — things participants brought up that weren't in your guide

<Tip>
  Low confidence on a decision usually means you need more data or a follow-up study on that topic.
</Tip>

## Deep Dive

Your results organized by moment. Each topic from your discussion guide gets its own section with a narrative summary, themes, quotes, and which participants contributed.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The Deep Dive view showing a moment breakdown with themes and quotes]
</Frame>

Good for understanding nuance in a specific topic, pulling quotes for a report, or comparing how different sessions handled the same question.

---

## Responses

Flips the view — organized by participant instead of by moment. See what each person said across every topic, their sentiment, and engagement level.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The Responses view showing a participant card with their answers across moments]
</Frame>

Useful for building participant personas or tracing one person's thinking through the full discussion. Filter by moment, participant, or session.

---

## Themes

Patterns across all your sessions. Convo groups related statements from different participants and different topics into themes.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: A theme card showing name, description, participant count, and supporting quotes]
</Frame>

Each theme shows a name, description, how many participants expressed it, which objectives it connects to, and direct quotes with attribution. Filter by objective, moment, or participant.

Themes are great for structuring a research report — use them as your main sections with quotes as evidence.

---

## Video clips

Convo pulls the best moments from recordings and turns them into short clips — strong opinions, emotional reactions, debates, clear articulations of a finding.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The clips gallery showing clip thumbnails with speaker names and topic labels]
</Frame>

Browse all clips or filter by topic, participant, or theme. Click any clip to preview it inline with the transcript.

Share clips with stakeholders who won't read a full report. A 30-second clip of a customer explaining their frustration is worth more than a page of analysis.
