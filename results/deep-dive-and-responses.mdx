While the Summary gives you the big picture, the Deep Dive and Responses tabs let you go granular. Use these views when you need to understand exactly how a specific topic played out or what a specific participant said.

## Deep Dive

The Deep Dive tab shows your study results organized by **moment** -- each topic from your discussion guide gets its own section.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The Deep Dive view showing a moment-by-moment breakdown with analysis, themes, and quotes for each topic]
</Frame>

For each moment, you will see:

- **How the topic played out** -- A narrative summary of what was discussed, including how the conversation evolved across sessions
- **Themes** -- Patterns that emerged specifically within this moment
- **Key quotes** -- The most relevant participant quotes for this topic
- **Participant coverage** -- Which participants contributed to this moment and what they said

This view is ideal for:

- Understanding the nuance within a specific topic
- Seeing how different sessions handled the same question
- Pulling quotes for a specific section of your research report

---

## Responses

The Responses tab flips the view. Instead of organizing by moment, it organizes by **participant**. You can see exactly what each person said across every moment in the study.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The Responses view showing per-participant cards with their answers to each moment, plus filter controls]
</Frame>

For each participant, you get:

- Their responses to each moment, pulled from the transcript
- Quotes attributed to them specifically
- Their overall sentiment and engagement level

### Filtering

Use the filter controls to narrow down the view:

- **By moment** -- See only responses to a specific topic
- **By participant** -- Focus on one or a few participants
- **By session** -- Compare how different session groups responded

<Tip>
  Use the Responses tab when building participant personas or when you need to trace a specific person's journey through the discussion.
</Tip>

---

## When to use each view

| Use case | Best view |
|----------|-----------|
| Understanding how a specific topic played out | Deep Dive |
| Pulling quotes for a specific section of a report | Deep Dive |
| Seeing what a specific participant said across all topics | Responses |
| Comparing how different participants answered the same question | Responses with moment filter |
| Building participant profiles or personas | Responses |
| Getting a narrative for a specific discussion topic | Deep Dive |
