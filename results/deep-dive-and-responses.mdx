The Summary gives you the big picture. Deep Dive and Responses let you get specific — how did a particular topic play out, or what did a particular participant actually say?

## Deep Dive

Shows your results organized by **moment** — each topic from your discussion guide gets its own section.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The Deep Dive view showing a moment-by-moment breakdown with analysis, themes, and quotes for each topic]
</Frame>

For each moment:

- A narrative summary of what was discussed and how it played out across sessions
- Themes that came up within this specific topic
- The most relevant quotes
- Which participants contributed and what they said

Good for understanding the nuance in a specific topic, comparing how different sessions handled the same question, or pulling quotes for a section of your report.

---

## Responses

Flips the view. Instead of organizing by moment, it organizes by **participant**. See exactly what each person said across every topic.

<Frame>
  [IMAGE: The Responses view showing per-participant cards with their answers to each moment, plus filter controls]
</Frame>

For each participant:

- Their responses to each moment, pulled from the transcript
- Quotes attributed to them
- Their overall sentiment and engagement level

### Filtering

Narrow things down by moment, participant, or session using the filter controls.

<Tip>
  The Responses tab is useful for building participant personas or tracing a specific person's journey through the discussion.
</Tip>

---

## When to use which

| You want to... | Use |
|----------|-----------|
| Understand how a specific topic played out | Deep Dive |
| Pull quotes for a section of a report | Deep Dive |
| See what one participant said across all topics | Responses |
| Compare how different people answered the same question | Responses + moment filter |
| Build participant profiles | Responses |
