## Data storage

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Study data — transcripts, analysis results, participant info — lives in managed databases with access controls.

---

## Recordings

Session recordings are in secure cloud storage. Access URLs are time-limited, meaning:

- Recordings aren't publicly accessible
- Links expire after a set period
- Only authenticated users with study access can view them

---

## Third-party services

Convo uses a few third-party services:

- **Transcription** — a speech-to-text service processes session audio
- **Text-to-speech** — generates the AI moderator's voice
- **Video processing** — creates clips from recordings
- **AI analysis** — large language models process transcripts to generate themes, summaries, and insights

Data sent to these services is used only for processing your sessions. It's not used to train third-party models.

---

## Data retention

Study data stays as long as your account is active. Archived studies remain accessible but aren't actively processed. Need something deleted? [Email us](mailto:support@useconvo.ai).

---

## Participant data

Participant data includes name, email, screener responses, session recordings, and transcript contributions. It's scoped to your study and only accessible to users with permission. Not shared across studies or with other customers.

---

## GDPR

If you're researching with participants in the EEA:

- Participants consent during signup before any data is collected
- They're told sessions will be recorded
- You can request deletion of participant data by contacting us
- Processing is covered by our data processing agreement

<Info>
  Need a DPA for your organization? [Email us](mailto:support@useconvo.ai) and we'll get one set up.
</Info>
