We take the security of your research data seriously. This page explains how Convo stores, processes, and protects your data.

## Data storage and encryption

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Your study data -- including transcripts, analysis results, and participant information -- is stored in secure, managed databases with access controls.

---

## Audio and video recordings

Session recordings are stored in secure cloud storage with time-limited access URLs. This means:

- Recordings are not publicly accessible
- Access links expire after a set period
- Only authenticated users with access to the study can view recordings

---

## Third-party services

Convo uses carefully selected third-party services for core functionality:

- **Transcription** -- Session audio is processed by a transcription service to generate accurate transcripts
- **Text-to-speech** -- Peggy's voice is generated using a text-to-speech service
- **Video processing** -- Clips and trailers are generated using video processing infrastructure
- **AI analysis** -- Transcripts are processed by large language models to generate themes, summaries, and insights

All third-party services are selected for their security practices and data handling policies. Data sent to these services is used only for processing your sessions and is not used to train third-party models.

---

## Data retention

Study data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you archive a study, the data remains accessible but is no longer actively processed. If you need data deleted, [contact our team](mailto:support@useconvo.ai).

---

## Participant data handling

Participant data includes:

- Name and email (provided during signup)
- Screener responses
- Session recordings (video and audio)
- Transcript contributions

This data is associated with your study and is accessible only to users who have permission to view that study. Participant data is not shared across studies or with other Convo customers.

---

## GDPR considerations

If you are conducting research with participants in the European Economic Area:

- Participants provide consent during the signup flow before any data is collected
- Participants are informed that sessions will be recorded
- You can request deletion of participant data by contacting our team
- Data processing is governed by our data processing agreement

<Info>
  If you need a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) for your organization, [contact us](mailto:support@useconvo.ai) and we will get one set up.
</Info>
