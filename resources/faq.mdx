---
title: FAQ
description: Frequently asked questions about Convo.
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many participants can be in a session?">
    Convo supports multi-participant focus group sessions. The exact number of participants per session depends on your study settings. You configure the group size when setting up your study. Smaller groups (3-5 participants) tend to produce richer discussion, while larger groups let you cover more perspectives per session.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I observe sessions in real time?">
    Currently, sessions are run by Peggy (the AI moderator) and recorded automatically. You can review the full recordings and transcripts as soon as a session ends. Real-time observation features are on our roadmap.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does analysis take?">
    Analysis typically takes a few minutes, depending on the number of sessions and total discussion time. The pipeline processes transcripts, identifies themes, generates clips, and creates your study trailer. You will be notified when results are ready.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit my study after launching?">
    You can edit some aspects of an active study, such as adding new recruitment rounds. However, certain settings like the screener cannot be changed once participants have started responding, as this would affect data consistency. We recommend finalizing your screener and discussion guide before activating your study.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if a participant has technical issues?">
    Before joining a session, every participant completes an AI-powered video and audio check. This catches most technical issues upfront. If a participant experiences problems during a session, the Director Agent monitors quality and Peggy can adapt the conversation flow accordingly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Convo for 1:1 interviews?">
    Yes. Set your group size to 1 participant per session, and Peggy will run a one-on-one interview following your discussion guide. The analysis and results work the same way -- you still get themes, clips, and a trailer across all your 1:1 sessions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the AI moderator handle off-topic responses?">
    Peggy is designed to keep conversations productive while remaining natural. If a participant goes off-topic, Peggy will acknowledge their comment and gently steer the conversation back to the current moment. If the off-topic comment is relevant to a later moment, Peggy may note it and return to it at the right time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What file formats can I upload for stimuli?">
    You can upload images (PNG, JPG, GIF), videos, and URLs as stimulus materials. These are attached to specific moments in your discussion guide and shown to participants automatically when Peggy reaches that moment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get access to B2B or professional recruitment?">
    General population panels are available directly in Convo. For professional or B2B recruitment (e.g., reaching decision-makers, developers, healthcare workers, or other specialized audiences), [contact our team](mailto:support@useconvo.ai) and we will set up a tailored recruitment plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I contact support?">
    Email us at [support@useconvo.ai](mailto:support@useconvo.ai). We typically respond within a few hours on business days.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
